The Best Movies According to Kelly

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Eternal Sunshine of a spotless mind

A thought provocating representation of heartbreak and mental illness. I love the way the shots have blurred the lines between memories and reality.

Kevin and Perry Go Large

A cheesy, funny over the top teenage movie from the 90's featuring an amazing soundtrack and the jokes are totally lame! An easy watch time after time.

Interstellar

An amazing big budget space flick, I loved the twists and turns and again an amazing soundtrack.
